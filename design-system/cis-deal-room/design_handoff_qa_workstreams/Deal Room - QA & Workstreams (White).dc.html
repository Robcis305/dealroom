05 / SYSTEM

Reference & handoff notes

The workstream and Q&A-status systems, plus the patterns these screens build on.
Workstream system
Seeded defaults, admin-editable per deal. Muted tones — used only on dots, tags & small fills, never large surfaces.
LegalContracts · corporate · IP#33322F
FinanceAudited financials · tax#5C5A54
TechnologySystems · data · security#84827A
HRPeople · comp · benefits#A8A69E
CommercialCustomers · pipeline · GTM#C7C5BD
Orthogonal to folders — an item keeps its single folder home and can carry many workstream tags. Membership grants access, layered as a union with existing folder access.
Q&A status lifecycle
Approval routing is derived from the engagement side (cisAdvisorySide), not configured per question.
New Assigned Answered Approved
NewSubmitted by the asker; routed to CIS / workstream responders.
AssignedAsker proposes an assignee; CIS confirms or reroutes.
AnsweredDesignee posts the proposed official answer; awaits the gate.
ApprovedCIS vets & releases; asker is notified, answer is pinned.
Overdue — flagged & filterable Public Private — recipient picker
